# Audio & Music

Sound cue playback, wave/sound bank registration, and the dynamic music system.

## Modules in this category

- **[MrxMusic](mrxmusic)** — The `MrxMusic` module is responsible for managing and controlling the music in the game.
- **[MrxSound](mrxsound)** — The `MrxSound` module is responsible for managing audio-related functionalities in the game.
- **[MrxSoundBanks](mrxsoundbanks)** — The `MrxSoundBanks` module is responsible for managing the loading and unloading of sound banks and wave banks in the game.
- **[MrxSoundCategories](mrxsoundcategories)** — The `MrxSoundCategories` module manages audio ducking, fading, and pitch adjustments for different sound categories in the game.
- **[MrxSubtitle](mrxsubtitle)** — The `MrxSubtitle` module manages the queueing and display of subtitles in the game.
